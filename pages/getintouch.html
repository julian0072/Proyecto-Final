Get in Touch

Jhonn Beale grew up documenting her life with her camera. The son of a cinematographer, both still and living pictures were big parts of his childhood.

His career in art started first as a student art gallery assistant. While working as a graphic designer, he started taking pictures of the newest and hottest exhibitions in town and posting them on his Instagram.

This quickly helped his gain traction and popularity, and soon he was asked to help with new gallery openings. Fast forward to today: you will most likely find her in her studio on Stanley Avenue, working on projects for both new and existing clients alike. You’ll be able to score a good cup of coffee if you stop by to say hi.
